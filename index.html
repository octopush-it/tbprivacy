Build. Protect. Connect. Win.

This is not a sleepy flashcard app.

Pick your workplace, create your player, and start taking on the kind of problems real IT teams see every day: DNS weirdness, cabling issues, VLAN trouble, DHCP headaches, routing questions, wireless complaints, security risks, latency, bandwidth bottlenecks, and more.

Every shift gives you choices. Work the queue, respond to incidents before they snowball, study when you need to sharpen up, and manage your energy so you do not burn out mid-shift.
